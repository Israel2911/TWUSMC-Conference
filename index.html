Conference Overview

Integrating the Human Factor in the Age of AI

How do we take back the human mind?
This conference explores how higher-order human thinking, critical consciousness, and emotional intelligence can steer the development and deployment of AI. Rather than allowing technology to dictate the terms of human experience, we emphasize reclaiming our unique faculties—curiosity, transparency, accountability, and relationship-building—as the foundation for trustworthy, reliable AI systems.

Topic Breakdown

  • Emotional Intelligence in Technology Leadership: The role of empathy, self-awareness, and social skills in building trustworthy AI and digital platforms. How emotional intelligence in leadership helps ensure technology aligns with real human needs.
  • Truth, Transparency, and Accountability: Pillars of Reliable AI: Building AI systems that are explainable and auditable. Human-driven oversight to reinforce truthfulness and prevent bias or misuse.
  • Critical Thinking & Higher-Order Consciousness: Strategies to foster curiosity, creativity, and independent analysis instead of offloading judgment to machines. How education and personal development cultivate higher-order beings who guide technology for society’s good.
  • Relationship-Centered AI: Ways technology can strengthen (not weaken) trust and relationships. Setting boundaries for AI in personal, professional, and societal spheres.
  • Human Oversight: Defining AI Quality: Human values and conscious decisions as the final arbiters of AI quality and impact. Collaborative models where AI augments but does not replace human expertise.
  • Building Trustworthy AI for Global Good: International perspectives on ethical AI; case studies of human-centered AI in action across cultures and industries.
Key Message: AI feeds information and amplifies capabilities, but it is human sensitivity, higher order thinking, and ethical grounding that must always lead the way.
Our opportunity is to ensure technology strengthens humanity and fosters a more truthful, transparent, and relational future.
