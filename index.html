Conference Overview

Integrating the Human Factor in the Age of AI

How do we take back the human mind?
This conference explores how higher-order human thinking can steer AI development.

Topic Breakdown

  • Emotional Intelligence: Empathy ensuring technology aligns with human needs.
  • Truth & Accountability: Human oversight preventing bias.
  • Critical Thinking: Fostering creativity over machine dependency.
  • Relationship-Centered AI: Strengthening human connections.
  • Human Oversight: Values defining AI quality.
  • Global Good: Ethical AI case studies worldwide.
AI amplifies, humans lead.
