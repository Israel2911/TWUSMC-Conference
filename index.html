Conference Overview

Integrating the Human Factor in the Age of AI

How do we take back the human mind?
This conference explores how higher-order human thinking, critical consciousness, and emotional intelligence can steer AI development. We emphasize reclaiming curiosity, transparency, accountability, and relationship-building as foundations for trustworthy AI.

Topic Breakdown

  • Emotional Intelligence in Technology Leadership: Empathy and social skills ensuring technology aligns with human needs.
  • Truth, Transparency, and Accountability: Human oversight preventing bias and ensuring explainable AI.
  • Critical Thinking & Higher-Order Consciousness: Fostering curiosity and creativity over machine dependency.
  • Relationship-Centered AI: Technology strengthening human trust and connections.
  • Human Oversight: Defining AI Quality: Human values as final arbiters of AI impact.
  • Building Trustworthy AI for Global Good: International case studies of ethical AI deployment.
Key Message: AI amplifies capabilities, but human sensitivity and ethical grounding must lead the way.
