Conference Overview

The 11th Annual Leadership Conference brings together global thought leaders to explore the critical intersection of human intelligence and artificial intelligence in the modern era. This landmark event focuses on "Fifth Intelligence" - the synergistic integration of human creativity, emotional intelligence, and ethical judgment with AI's computational power and pattern recognition. Featuring keynote presentations, interactive workshops, and international industry panels, the conference addresses how leaders can harness AI as a force multiplier while preserving the uniquely human elements that drive innovation, trust, and meaningful progress. Participants will gain practical frameworks for AI-human collaboration across industries, cultures, and generations. Join us for transformative insights from India, Canada, Thailand, Iran, Nigeria, and China - united by a shared vision for responsible AI leadership in the global economy.
